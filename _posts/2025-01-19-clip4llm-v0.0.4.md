Pre-built binaries are available for all major platforms:

- **Linux**: `clip4llm-v0.0.4-linux-amd64.tar.gz`, `clip4llm-v0.0.4-linux-arm64.tar.gz`, or `clip4llm-v0.0.4-linux-386.tar.gz`

Download the appropriate asset from the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/UnitVectorY-Labs/clip4llm/releases/tag/v0.0.4). Each binary includes checksum files for verification.

Upgrading is straightforward:

1. Download the binary for your platform
2. Replace your existing `clip4llm` binary
3. That's it—no configuration changes needed

Your existing `.clip4llm` configuration files and command-line flags remain fully compatible.

If you prefer to build from source, you'll need Go 1.23.5 or later:

