## Core Capabilities

Since this is our first release, we are introducing a comprehensive suite of features to get you started:

- **Dual Execution Modes**: Choose between a streamlined CLI for scripting and a rich, interactive TUI (Terminal User Interface) for visual configuration.
- **Intelligent Browser Discovery**: No more hunting for paths; gowebshot automatically finds Chrome or Chromium installations on your system.
- **Precision Viewport Control**: Use built-in presets like widescreen (1920×1080), desktop, square, and portrait (1080×1350), or define your own custom dimensions.
- **Advanced Page Tuning**: Fine-tune your captures with adjustable zoom levels, vertical scroll offsets, and configurable load delays to ensure animations have finished and the page has settled.
- **Smart File Management**: Avoid accidental overwrites with automatic numeric suffixing and full control over output directories and filenames.

## Why it matters

For developers, designers, and researchers, the ability to capture consistent, high-quality webpage snapshots without leaving the terminal is a game-changer.
